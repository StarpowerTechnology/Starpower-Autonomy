## ? 3. Understanding the Data

Most apps and platforms display similar data points. Here’s what to look for:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
 --- |
| Solar Input (W) | How much energy your panel is producing |
| Battery Voltage (V) | Indicates charging status or low voltage risks |
